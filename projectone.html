This was the first big project that we made in my Introduction to Programming class. The prompt for the project was: create an album cover for an album/song that represents your feeling and emotions while listening to the song. I chose to make an cover for the song "Mr. Blue Sky" by Electric Light Orchestra. I didn't hear this song until I watch Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 2. I really like this song because it is peaceful and everytime I listen to this song I feel Happy. I chose to make the cover for the song a blue sky (like it says in the song) with rolling clouds and a bright midday sun. This song just makes me think of a bright sunny summer day so I chose to make the cover just that. The most difficult stuggle I went through while making this sketch was the clouds. I wanted the clouds to be different every time they passed by so I created one singular function that randomly created the shape and size of the clouds. This was the most difficult for me because it was the first time I created such a randimized function. I hope you enjoyed the sketch!
