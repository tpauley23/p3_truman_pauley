"W" = Up, "S" = Down, "A" = Left, "D" = Right

For this sketch I added different elements copied from previous sketches that I made on p5.js I made a watermelon that bounced around the screen and I also used an assortment of rectanges and ellipses to make a recreation of character from the popular game Amoung Us. In this sketch you can control the characture by using the classic MineCraft controles of "W," "A," "S," and "D" Keys to move the Character. The watermelon will bounce around the screen at different speeds and you have to use your skills of "Dodge, Duck, Dip, Dive, Dodge" (...) to avoid the watermelon! If You get hit by the watermelon it GAME OVER! The longer you stay alive the higher your score gets! You can see your score in the top left corner! The longer you stay alive the Faster the water melon goes! Have Fun Playing! The highest score that I ever got was a 38! Also, You can go off the screen just know that you won't be getting any points while your off the screen. Have Fun!
