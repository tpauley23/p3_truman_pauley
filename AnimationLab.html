During the first unit of Introduction to Programming Class, we learned about animation. We were then assigned a mini project called the Animation Lab in which we were supposed to make a sketch that incorperated animation. I first made on of those loading screens that had a logo bounce around. Then during one of our classes, I put a sketch of a watermelon I had made the previous week instead of the basic ball. I wanted to make it spin so I spent a long time making the watermelon both bounce around the screen and spin. I ended up having to use alot of math involving radians but I think that the finished product was worth it. The effect of the spinning wattermellon creates spirals that go across the page! This is one of my favority sketchs and it always statisfies me when the whole screen gets filled up!
